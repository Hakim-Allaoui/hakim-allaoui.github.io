Data Deletion Instructions for Feedback Master

At Feedback Master, we understand the importance of your privacy and give you full control over your personal data and account information. Below are the methods through which you can request data deletion:

Delete Your Account and Data via Email

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please send us an email with your request. You will need to provide us with your account details (such as email address or user ID) to verify ownership and process the deletion. We will respond to your inquiry within 2 days and guide you through the steps necessary to securely delete your data.

Contact us for data deletion:

  • Email: khedam.console@outlook.com

Please note that once your data is deleted, all information related to your account will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

In-App Account and Data Deletion

You also have the option to delete your account directly through the Feedback Master app. This section will guide you through the process of deleting your account using the app interface.

Steps to delete your account in-app:

  1. Open the app and navigate to the Settings menu.
  2. Select ‘Account Settings’.
  3. Scroll to the ‘Delete Account’ section.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion.




Please Note:

  • Deleting your account will remove all data associated with it, including feedback, ratings, and personal information.
  • Once deleted, your account cannot be restored, and you will lose access to all services provided by Feedback Master without the possibility of recovery.

For any questions or additional assistance regarding the data deletion process, feel free to contact our support team at khedam.console@outlook.com
